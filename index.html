Net Art Project

It all began with the doll.

On Christmas day, Lily was given a present from her grandmother. Wrapped in an old, christmas wrapping paper decorated with christmas trees and ornaments, the box contained a teddy bear-sized doll holding a letter.

Looking at the doll, her clothes were old and torn, the color from her cheeks faded, and those gleaming eyes were lifeless. Slumped against the walls of the box, the doll looked ominous and creepy. Its eyes stared back at her, waiting for Lily to touch her.

Displeased, Lily felt that she was too old for dolls, especially old ones that smelled of her grandmother. Without taking another peek, she sealed the box and stored it in the very back of her closet so that she would never have to see it again.

At first, everything felt normal. But suddenly, she began to hear footsteps and a child’s laugh echo throughout the house.

Shaken, Lily carefully searched the house for the source of the laughter but found nothing.

“Do not worry Lily. It is nothing. It must be your own imagination. Maybe it was just a group of children passing by the house. Yes, that should be it,” she thought to herself.

Days passed and nothing happened.

One day she opened her closet to find that the doll was missing. Searching around, she found her in the abandoned attic full of family heirlooms, antiques, and forgotten toys.

She found that to be odd but thought nothing of it. Maybe she had placed it there one random day.

Slowly, more creepy things started to happened; the furniture was moved every day and her things were constantly misplaced. Coming to the conclusion that the doll could be haunted, she decides to go visit her grandmother to return the doll.

She goes to her grandmother’s house and knocks on her door to be greeted with no answer. She turns the doorknob to find the door unlocked and enters her grandmother’s house.

“Grandma? Are you there? Anyone home?”

Silence.

Walking around the house, she explored every room and called out to her grandmother. She went upstairs and found her grandmother, dead on her bathroom floor.

After her grandmother’s funeral, Lily is still stuck with the doll, never given the chance to ask her grandmother about it. Gradually, more paranormal things start to happen in her house and it progressively gets worse.

Door slams, creaky floors, scratches through the walls, voices…

She decides to throw the doll away, believing that everything paranormal is linked to the doll; she just wants her life to be normal again.

Late one night, she was webcamming with her friend when the lights suddenly went out in her house. She decides to check it out and noticed that her front door was open.

After closing and locking the door, she decides to go to the kitchen and arm herself with a knife for protection. When she entered the kitchen, all of her drawers were opened and pulled out.

Scared, she races back to her room and told her friend what happened. Her friend had a scared look on her face and told Lily to turn around.

As soon as she did, she sees the doll behind her.

The End (?)
