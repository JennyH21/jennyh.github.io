Artist Statement

Art is the best. When someone asks me why I make art, I ask them: WHY NOT.

For my first project, I decided to make a video with the concepts involving dreams and nightmares. The materials I used was my photography camera, my phone, and my laptop. Half of my video was filmed on my camera and the other half was filmed on my phone. I filmed both during the day and at night, and I used music from a free sound sharing website to include in my video in order to enhance it for the viewers. For my video process, I decided to edit it all on Premiere with video transitions to make it look more smooth and clean. I had to edit my clips around and place them in a certain order so that my video will relate to my concept.
